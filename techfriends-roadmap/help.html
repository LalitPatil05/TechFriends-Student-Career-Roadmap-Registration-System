Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my progress in a course?

To reset your progress in a course, go to the course page and click on the settings icon (three dots) in the top right corner. Select "Reset Progress" from the dropdown menu. Please note that this action cannot be undone and will remove all your completed lessons and quiz scores for that course.

Can I download courses for offline viewing?

Yes, most courses can be downloaded for offline viewing. Look for the download icon next to each lesson or module. You'll need the TechFriends mobile app to access downloaded content offline. Some courses with interactive coding exercises may have limited offline functionality.

How do I enroll in a new course?

To enroll in a new course, navigate to the "Roadmaps" section from the main navigation. Browse through available courses and click on any course that interests you. On the course page, click the "Enroll" button to add it to your learning path. Some advanced courses may require completing prerequisites first.

What happens if I fall behind in a course?

Don't worry! Our platform is designed for flexible learning. You can learn at your own pace. If you need to catch up, you can review previous lessons and use the "Study Plan" feature to create a customized schedule. You can also reach out to our community or mentors for additional support.

How do I update my email address?

To update your email address, go to your profile settings by clicking on your avatar in the top right corner and selecting "Settings". In the "Account" tab, you'll find the option to change your email address. You'll need to verify your new email address through a confirmation link sent to your new email.

The code editor is not working properly. What should I do?

If you're experiencing issues with the code editor, try these steps: 1) Refresh the page, 2) Clear your browser cache, 3) Try using a different browser (Chrome or Firefox recommended), 4) Check if JavaScript is enabled in your browser settings. If the issue persists, contact our technical support team with details about the problem.
